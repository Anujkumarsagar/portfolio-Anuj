# Quick Start - Upload Your 3D Car Model

## TL;DR - Get Your Car Spinning in 2 Minutes

### Step 1: Convert Your Model (if needed)
If you have a `.obj` file:
1. Go to: https://sandbox.babylonjs.com/
2. Click "Import" → Select your `.obj` file
3. File → Export → GLB
4. Save as `car.glb`

### Step 2: Upload Your Model
1. Open your project folder
2. Navigate to: `public/models/`
3. Paste your `car.glb` file there
4. Done!

### Step 3: See It Live
1. Refresh your browser
2. Your 3D car now appears in the hero section!
3. Try clicking and dragging to rotate
4. Scroll to zoom in/out
